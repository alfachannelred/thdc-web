OUR INSPIRATION

Cocooned in the foothills of Western Ghats you will find one of the awe-inspiring locations ever. An ideal place to reinvent your inner silence and rejuvenate your subtle energies. Welcome to The Hill District Club. THDC is more than a holiday retreat. The club offers an excellent opportunity to rediscover the missing spirit in your life. With beautiful locations, best facilities, splendid cuisines and pleasing hospitality THDC showcases many facilities under one roof.

 

A PANORAMIC VIEW

Enjoy the traditional artistic performance to lighten your evenings or cycle a less trodden path to explore the picturesque landscape - THDC offers a palette of experiences to make your stay memorable. Sprawled over 3.5 acres of green land, the club boasts many facilities such as mini convention centre to pristine swimming pools.

 

AMBIENCE

An occasional cry of hornbill echoes across the breathtaking landscape intertwined with coffee plantations and paddy fields. Though cut-off from the ordinary existence you will be surprised to find yourself blend in no time with the spotless nature here. The Hill District club is a major initiative to bring back nature into our lives, a far cry from the hustle and bustle of today’s world.
